The ELEMNT GPS bike computer works as a central cockpit, integrating with existing sensors, training platforms, and hardware through open ANT+ and Bluetooth standards rather than proprietary ones. It supports speed, cadence, power meters, heart rate monitors, electronic drivetrains (Shimano Di2, SRAM eTap AXS, Campagnolo EPS), radar, and newer sensors for hydration (Flowbio, hDrop), breathing (Tymewear VitalPro), and body temperature (CORE), and syncs with apps including TrainingPeaks, intervals.icu, Komoot, Strava, and RideWithGPS.
